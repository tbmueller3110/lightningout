    Overview

    Capital™ Electra™ X is a powerful, cloud-native Electrical CAD that offers speed, efficiency and convenience to complete your electrical designs 300-500% faster, no training, download nor installation required. Just a secure sign-in and you’re in business.

    With a team based dashboard, create and share drawings, symbols, circuits and other assets automatically with the entire company without complicated setup and expensive infrastructure. Work from home, in the office or at site, and be able to access the latest version instantly, without worrying about emails and version control. Collaborate with stakeholders easily with customizable permission controls to ensure your project is a success.

    Capital Electra X is extremely easy to use, with just a drag and drop interface, but still contains advanced automation tools like Autowiring, Intelligent Automatic Numbering, Smart Circuit Reuse, Generating Bills of Materials, Generating Panel Layout, Generating Terminals and many, many more. Use out of the box, with thousands of default symbols and circuits including Electrical, Pneumatic, Hydraulic and P&ID, and still be able to create your own symbols easily, to be shared with team mates without doing the same thing over and over again.
     

    Benefits

    Why Capital Electra X? 

    1. Easy Access
    • Use on any device, anytime, anywhere
    • No download and installation, fully browser-based
    • Get started immediately with just a single sign-in

    2. Seamless Collaboration
    • Team-based dashboard: Create multiple teams for different projects with customizable access control and easily manage various CAD resources in one place.
    • Share symbols among team members without complicated setup and configuration
    • Real-time collaborative editing: Changes are automatically synchronised to all open editors, irrespective of where they are.
    • Automatic Syncing and Updating: No more manual file savings; get the latest changes instantly.
    • Built-in version control: Gain the flexibility to roll back to any previous version of your drawings.
    • Built-in chat system: Easy communication without needing to switch between applications.

    3. Advanced Automation Tools
    • Autowiring
    • Intelligent Automatic Numbering
    • Smart Circuit Reuse
    • Reusable prefab circuits
    • Symbol and Wire Inheritance
    • Real-time reference window
    • Generate panel layout
    • Generate reports like Bill of materials (BOM), cross reference, and table of contents

    4. Ready-to-use symbols or enjoy the flexibility to create one easily
